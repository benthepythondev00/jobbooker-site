HVAC questions

Does it handle true emergencies?

It acknowledges them in under a minute, delivers your approved safety wording, and escalates to your on-call contact. It does not promise arrival times.

Can it book maintenance-plan visits differently from repairs?

Yes. Visit types, durations, notice windows, and availability are configured per your playbook during onboarding.

What if our calendar is full?

The booking stays unconfirmed and escalated. The lead is told the time was not confirmed, and you get the failure path—no imaginary bookings.
