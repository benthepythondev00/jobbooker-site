Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Overview

JobBooker provides managed lead-intake, qualification, delivery, scheduling, and reporting services for home-service businesses. This policy explains what we collect through getjobbooker.com and the JobBooker service, why we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

Audit requests

We collect the name, company, email, trade, optional website, lead-source names, attribution fields, and—only if you opt in to SMS—your mobile number. The optional audit CSV is processed inside your browser. Raw CSV rows are not uploaded to JobBooker; only aggregate counts and response-time metrics are submitted.

Public sandbox

The sandbox stores the selected trade, the messages you enter, a one-way network identifier used for rate limiting, and session state. It does not request contact fields and rejects phone numbers and email addresses. Do not enter names, street addresses, or other personal information. A session becomes inaccessible after 15 minutes and expired sessions are removed by our retention process.

Client lead workflows

When a business activates a pilot, JobBooker may process lead contact details, source identifiers, consent metadata, qualification answers, messages, delivery status, booking status, and calendar event identifiers on that business’s instructions. The client remains responsible for the lawful collection of its lead data and for its customer notices.

Technical data

We may process request time, IP-derived rate-limit values, browser or server logs, provider response identifiers, and error information needed to secure, operate, and troubleshoot the service. Application logs are designed not to include message bodies or contact details.

How we use information

  • Respond to audit and pilot requests.
  • Operate configured lead-intake, delivery, qualification, booking, and reporting workflows.
  • Detect duplicates, enforce consent and opt-outs, prevent abuse, and validate signed requests.
  • Monitor reliability, retry failed deliveries, escalate permanent failures, and improve the service.
  • Meet legal, security, accounting, and compliance obligations.

SMS and mobile information

SMS is optional. If you check the SMS consent box, you agree to receive messages from JobBooker about your request. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

We do not sell or share mobile numbers, SMS consent records, or SMS opt-in data with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. We may disclose the minimum information needed to messaging and infrastructure providers that deliver the service on our behalf. Opt-out suppression records may be retained as needed to honor your request.

Service providers and disclosure

We may use hosting, email, messaging, calendar, security, and infrastructure providers to deliver the service. They receive only the information needed for their role and are not authorized by us to use it for their own marketing. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights or safety, or as part of a business transaction subject to appropriate safeguards.

Retention

Raw lead messages and contact details are configured for a default 90-day retention period, unless a shorter pilot setting, an active customer relationship, a legal obligation, or a security need requires a different period. Audit contact details are normally redacted after 90 days. Aggregate delivery, booking, and performance records may be retained longer because they no longer contain the raw conversation or contact fields. Opt-out suppression data may be retained to prevent unwanted messages.

Security

Controls include client-scoped keys, signed webhook validation, payload limits, idempotency, least-privilege provider credentials, redacted application logs, encrypted transport, and restricted administrative access. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can decline SMS, reply STOP to an SMS, or ask us to access, correct, or delete information associated with your audit request. If JobBooker processed your information for one of our pilot clients, contact that business first because it controls the lead relationship. We will assist the client where appropriate.

Children

JobBooker is a business service and is not directed to children under 13. Do not submit children’s information.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and will change the effective date above when we do. Questions or privacy requests can be sent to ben@jobbooker-team.com.
